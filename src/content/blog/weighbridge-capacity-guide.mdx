Choosing the right **weighbridge capacity** is about matching the scale to the
heaviest fully loaded vehicles you will weigh — with a sensible safety margin.

## Start with your heaviest vehicle

Work out the gross weight of your heaviest fully loaded vehicle. The weighbridge
should comfortably exceed this, not just meet it.

## Add a safety margin

A safety margin protects the structure and load cells from overload and extends
service life. The right margin depends on your traffic and how close real loads
run to the maximum.

## Match platform length too

Capacity isn't the only factor — the platform must be long enough for your
longest vehicles to sit fully on the deck.
