A weighbridge is only as good as its foundation. Getting the **civil work**
right is essential for accuracy and long service life.

## Site selection

Choose a location with stable ground, room for vehicles to approach and leave
safely, and access for installation and future service.

## Drainage

Good drainage keeps the foundation and load cells dry. This matters especially
for pit-mounted designs; pitless designs are often simpler where groundwater is
a concern.

## Foundation to specification

The foundation must be built to the weighbridge's specification — dimensions,
reinforcement, and load-cell mounting points — so the structure performs as
designed.
