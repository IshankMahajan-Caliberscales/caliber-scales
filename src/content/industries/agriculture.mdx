## Weighing solutions for agriculture

Agriculture weighs a huge range of goods — grain, produce, fertiliser, and other
inputs — and often does it in seasonal bursts, outdoors, and sometimes at sites
that change from one year to the next. Fair, accurate weighing matters for
procurement and trade, while flexibility and cost matter for seasonal or smaller
operations.

Caliber Scales offers agriculture both permanent and relocatable options:
[instant mobile weighbridges](/weighbridges/mobile/) that install in hours with
no foundation, [track weighing weighbridges](/weighbridges/track/) as an
economical choice for lighter vehicles, and fixed
[surface mounted weighbridges](/weighbridges/surface-mounted/) for permanent, high-volume sites.

## Common challenges we solve

- **Seasonality** — relocatable weighbridges for sites that move or peak.
- **Fair trade** — calibrated, accurate weighing for procurement.
- **Outdoor conditions** — durable equipment for dust and weather.
- **Budget** — right-sized, cost-effective options for smaller sites.
