## Weighing solutions for logistics & transport

For logistics and transport businesses, weighing is a commercial control point.
Every inbound and outbound vehicle has to be weighed quickly and accurately —
for fair billing, for overload compliance, and for clean records. Slow or
unreliable weighing means queues, disputes, and lost time at exactly the moments
your operation can least afford them.

As a manufacturer, Caliber Scales supplies and services the full weighing chain
for logistics operations: heavy-duty [weighbridges](/weighbridges/) for vehicles
at the gate and yard, [logistics scales](/products/logistics-scales/) with
volumetric dimensioning for parcels and pallets in despatch, and
[unmanned weighbridge automation](/weighbridges/unmanned/) for high-throughput
sites that need to weigh around the clock.

## Common challenges we solve

- **Gate congestion** — fast, reliable weighing (and automation) keeps vehicles
  moving instead of queuing.
- **Billing disputes** — accurate, calibrated weighing and clear records remove
  the arguments over weights.
- **Overload risk** — knowing true gross weights helps you avoid penalties and
  keep loads legal.
- **Manual data entry** — integration and dimensioning cut the errors that creep
  in from manual recording.
