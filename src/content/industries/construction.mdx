## Weighing solutions for construction & infrastructure

Construction and infrastructure work depends on accurate weighing of aggregates,
sand, cement, steel, and the vehicles that carry them — frequently at temporary
or fast-changing sites. Equipment has to be rugged, accurate, and quick to set up.

Caliber Scales supplies projects with [instant mobile weighbridges](/weighbridges/mobile/)
that install in hours with no foundation, and permanent
[surface mounted](/weighbridges/surface-mounted/) and
[pit type weighbridges](/weighbridges/pit-type/) for established yards.

## Common challenges we solve

- **Temporary sites** — relocatable weighbridges that move with the project.
- **Heavy, dusty flows** — durable equipment built for site conditions.
- **Cost control** — accurate material weighing for billing and stock.
- **Fast start-up** — quick-deploy options for urgent requirements.
