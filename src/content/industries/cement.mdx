## Weighing solutions for the cement industry

Few environments are tougher on a weighbridge than a cement plant — relentless
heavy traffic, abrasive dust, and despatch volumes where every weighment is
revenue. Weighing has to be rugged, accurate, and available, because downtime on
the bridge can stall the whole despatch operation.

Caliber Scales builds cement plants weighing they can rely on:
[pit type weighbridges](/weighbridges/pit-type/) with reinforced-concrete decks for heavy,
continuous use, [surface mounted weighbridges](/weighbridges/surface-mounted/) that avoid
water-logging, and [unmanned automation](/weighbridges/unmanned/) to keep
despatch moving with fewer operators.
