## Weighing solutions for industrial terminals & hubs

Terminals, depots, and logistics hubs move vehicles continuously, and every
minute at the gate counts. Weighing needs to be fast, accurate, and automated so
traffic keeps flowing — ideally around the clock and with minimal manpower.

Caliber Scales equips terminals with
[unmanned automated weighbridges](/weighbridges/unmanned/) (RFID, barriers, and
kiosk operation) built on durable
[surface mounted](/weighbridges/surface-mounted/) or
[pit type weighbridges](/weighbridges/pit-type/).

## Common challenges we solve

- **High throughput** — automation that keeps the gate moving.
- **24/7 operation** — unattended weighing with minimal staff.
- **Accuracy** — calibrated, reliable weighing for every movement.
- **Security** — auditable records with photo capture.
