## Weighing solutions for the steel industry

Steel making is heavy, hot, and continuous — and the weighing equipment has to
match. From raw-material intake at the gate to finished-goods despatch, and from
craned loads on the shop floor to weighing near furnaces, steel plants need
rugged equipment that stays accurate in punishing conditions.

Caliber Scales supplies steel operations with durable
[pit type](/weighbridges/pit-type/) and [surface mounted weighbridges](/weighbridges/surface-mounted/) for
vehicle weighing, and [crane scales](/products/crane-scales/) —
including heat-resistant models — for suspended and high-temperature loads.

## Common challenges we solve

- **Heavy, continuous duty** — high-capacity weighbridges built to last.
- **Craned loads** — hanging scales that weigh suspended loads accurately.
- **High temperatures** — heat-resistant crane scales for hot areas.
- **End-to-end accuracy** — reliable weighing from intake to despatch.
