## Weighing solutions for the chemical industry

Chemical processing puts a premium on accuracy and safety. Much of the weighing
happens in the vessel — during dosing, mixing, blending, or thermal processes —
and the equipment has to suit the materials and the environment, which can be
corrosive or hazardous.

Caliber Scales designs
[customised tank and vessel weighing systems](/products/tank-weighing-systems/)
that weigh contents in place for liquids, solids, semi-solids, and gases, and
supplies stainless [platform scales](/products/platform-scales/) for drums and
container handling. Systems can be retrofitted to existing vessels or engineered
into new installations.

## Common challenges we solve

- **In-vessel weighing** — measure contents in place without a separate step.
- **Material range** — systems for liquids, solids, semi-solids, and gases.
- **Batching accuracy** — process-integrated weighing for consistent formulation.
- **Tough environments** — construction suited to corrosive or hazardous areas.
