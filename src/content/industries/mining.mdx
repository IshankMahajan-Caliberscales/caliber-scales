## Weighing solutions for mining & quarrying

Mines and quarries put weighing equipment through some of the harshest duty
there is — abrasive dust, vibration, and a steady stream of heavily loaded
vehicles, often at remote sites where reliability is everything. On top of that,
weighing data underpins despatch, stock control, and royalty records, so
accuracy is non-negotiable.

Caliber Scales builds weighbridges for exactly these conditions:
[pit type weighbridges](/weighbridges/pit-type/) with hard-wearing reinforced-concrete
decks, [surface mounted weighbridges](/weighbridges/surface-mounted/) that avoid water-logging
on uneven ground, and [unmanned automation](/weighbridges/unmanned/) for secure,
round-the-clock weighing.
