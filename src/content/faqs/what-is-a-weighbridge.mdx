question: What is a weighbridge?
answer: A weighbridge — also called a truck scale or Dharam Kanta — is a large weighing machine that measures the total weight of a vehicle and its load. Unlike axle weighers or weighing pads that measure pressure on individual wheels or axles, a weighbridge weighs the whole vehicle at once as it stands on the platform. Caliber weighbridges are available from 5 MT to 100 MT in surface mounted, pit type, instant mobile, track, and unmanned configurations.
