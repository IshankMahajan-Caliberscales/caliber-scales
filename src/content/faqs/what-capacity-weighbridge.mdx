question: What weighbridge capacity do I need?
answer: Caliber weighbridges are available from 5 MT to 100 MT. The right capacity is set by the heaviest fully loaded vehicle you will weigh, plus a sensible safety margin, and the platform must be long enough for your longest vehicles. Tell us your vehicle types and typical loads and we will recommend the right capacity and platform size.
