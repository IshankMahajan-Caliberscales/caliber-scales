question: What is a Dharam Kanta?
answer: Dharam Kanta is the common Indian term for a weighbridge — a weighing instrument long used to measure the weight of road and rail vehicles and their loads. A modern electronic Dharam Kanta works just like any weighbridge; the vehicle stands on a platform supported by load cells, which measure the total weight and show it on a digital indicator.
