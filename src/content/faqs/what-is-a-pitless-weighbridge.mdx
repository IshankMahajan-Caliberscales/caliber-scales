question: What is a pitless weighbridge?
answer: A pitless weighbridge — also called a surface mounted weighbridge — is an above-ground truck scale mounted on a raised foundation with approach ramps, so no pit needs to be excavated. It is quick to install, easier to clean and maintain, and well suited to sites with drainage or groundwater concerns.
