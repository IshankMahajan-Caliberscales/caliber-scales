## Crane & hanging scales for suspended loads

Caliber Scales manufactures industrial **crane (hanging) scales** that calculate
the weight of a suspended load — used with overhead or EOT cranes, chains, and
shackles. Built for harsh environments, they feature a **large display readable
from a distance**, a **wireless remote with around 20 m range**, a
**rechargeable battery**, and a **dismountable hook** that fits standard lifting
equipment. Capacities run from **1,000 kg to 30,000 kg**.

## Industrial crane scales

The standard range suspends between your crane and the load to weigh it in the
air — ideal where placing items on a platform is impractical. Rugged
construction and a long-runtime rechargeable battery keep them working through
demanding shifts.

## Heat-resistant crane scales

For heat-intensive processes such as steel and foundry work, our **heat-resistant
crane scales are protected up to 1600°C**, so you can weigh hot loads safely and
accurately.

## LPG hanging scales

Our **LPG hanging scale** is purpose-built for cylinder weighing: up to **50 kg**
capacity, with a dismountable hook, a top handle for easy lifting, a rechargeable
battery, and a seamless finish with a plastic dust and moisture cover.

## Why choose Caliber crane scales

- **Weigh suspended loads** without a platform
- **Large display** and **~20 m wireless remote**
- **Rechargeable battery** for mobile use
- **Robust** for harsh industrial conditions
- **Specialised variants** — heat-resistant and LPG
