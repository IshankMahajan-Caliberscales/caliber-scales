## Hanging scales for suspended weighing

Caliber Scales manufactures **hanging scales** that weigh a load while it is
suspended from a hook — ideal where placing items on a platform is impractical.
With a **dismountable hook**, a clear display, and a **rechargeable battery**,
they suit material handling, workshops, and cylinder weighing.

## LPG hanging scales

Our **LPG hanging scale** is purpose-built for cylinder weighing: up to **50 kg**
capacity, with a dismountable hook, a top handle for easy lifting, a rechargeable
battery, and a seamless finish with a plastic dust and moisture cover.

## Why choose Caliber hanging scales

- **Weigh suspended loads** without a platform
- **Dismountable hook** for standard lifting points
- **Rechargeable battery** for mobile use
- **Dedicated LPG** cylinder model

Hanging scales suit warehousing, material handling, workshops, and LPG cylinder
