## Table top scales for counter and bench weighing

Table top scales are **compact scales that fit on a bench or workstation**,
designed for industries, commercial establishments, retail counters, and
warehouses. Caliber Scales manufactures them from **1 kg to 50 kg**, in
**Class II and Class III** accuracy, and in **metal, stainless steel, or ABS**
bodies — so they can be deployed even in hazardous environments that might damage
other scale types.

## Variants

We offer three specialised table top scale types:

- **Weighing + counting** — count identical parts by weight
- **Price-computing** — calculate price from weight for retail
- **Baby-weighing** — gentle, accurate weighing for clinics and homes

## Why choose Caliber table top scales

- **Compact** and easy to place on any counter or bench
- **Class II/III** accuracy for dependable everyday weighing
- **MS, SS, or ABS** body to match your environment
- **Specialised variants** for counting, retail pricing, and baby weighing
