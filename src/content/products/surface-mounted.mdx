## Surface mounted weighbridge — built for India's toughest sites

A **surface mounted weighbridge** (also called a **pitless weighbridge**) is an
electronic truck scale installed above ground on a raised foundation, with
approach ramps at either end. Because there is no pit to excavate, it is faster
and cheaper to install than a pit type design and avoids the drainage and
water-logging problems that pits can suffer in Indian conditions.

As a manufacturer since 2001, Caliber Scales builds surface mounted weighbridges
from **5 MT to 100 MT**, engineered for accurate, reliable weighing under
continuous heavy traffic.

## Why choose a surface mounted design

- **No excavation.** A raised foundation replaces a deep pit, cutting civil cost
  and lead time and getting your weighbridge operational sooner.
- **No water-logging.** With the deck and load cells above ground, a surface
  mounted weighbridge eliminates the standing-water problems that can affect pits
  — ideal for flood-prone and high-groundwater sites.
- **Easy maintenance.** The open structure underneath gives technicians clear
  access to load cells and wiring for inspection and service.
- **Versatile siting.** Hilly, rocky, or uneven ground that makes a pit
  impractical is rarely a problem for a surface mounted weighbridge.

## How it works

Vehicles drive up the ramps onto a robust steel deck supported by load cells.
Each load cell converts the weight it carries into an electrical signal; the
combined signal is processed by a **pre-calibrated digital indicator** and shown
on an LED, GLCD, or TFT display. The reading can be printed, stored, and fed into
your records or weighing software.

## Instrumentation and options

Every Caliber weighbridge can be configured with the instrumentation your
operation needs:

- Pre-calibrated indicators with intelligent terminals
- Connectivity over **RS232, RS485, Ethernet, and USB**
- IP cameras for photo capture of each weighment
- Vehicle-positioning guidance
- Weighbridge software for ticketing and reporting
- Lightning-protection systems for exposed sites

## Applications

Surface mounted weighbridges are a popular choice for logistics and transport
yards, cement and aggregate plants, mining and quarrying, and any site where
drainage, groundwater, or terrain rules out a pit. They are equally at home in
warehousing and manufacturing despatch.
