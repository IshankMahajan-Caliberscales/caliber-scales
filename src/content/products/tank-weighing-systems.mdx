## Customised tank weighing systems

Caliber Scales designs and manufactures **customised tank, hopper, and vessel
weighing systems** that provide accurate weighing information for material held
in a vessel — without moving it to a separate scale. Load cells are mounted
**above or below** the vessel and integrated with your process, so you can weigh
in place during filling, dosing, mixing, or discharge.

Capacities range **from as low as 100 kg to as high as 100 MT**, and the system
can be **retrofitted to existing vessels** or engineered into new installations.

## What it can weigh

Tank weighing systems handle **liquids, solids, semi-solids, and gaseous
material**, across a wide range of processes:

- Inline weighing systems
- Mixing, kneading, blending, and dissolving tanks
- Heating and cooling (thermal) tanks

## Why choose a Caliber tank weighing system

- **Weigh in place** — no separate weighing step or material transfer
- **Fits tight spaces** — flexible mounting for constrained installations
- **Process-ready** — integrates with batching and process control
- **Retrofit or new** — adapts to existing vessels or new builds
- **Custom-engineered** to your vessel, material, and environment

Tank weighing is widely used in chemical and process industries, food and pharma
