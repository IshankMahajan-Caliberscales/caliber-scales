## High precision analytical balances

High precision analytical balances are used to measure the weight of **smaller
items or substances within a specific range at a very low rate of error**.
Caliber Scales supplies laboratory-grade balances that read in **grams,
milligrams, and micrograms**, for applications where accuracy and repeatability
are essential.

## Where they're used

- **Laboratories** — sample preparation, testing, and analysis
- **Jewellery stores** — accurate weighing of precious metals and stones
- **Research & development centres** — precise measurement of small quantities
- **Quality control** — verifying weights to fine tolerances

## Why choose Caliber analytical balances

- **High precision** for very small masses
- **Low error** for dependable, repeatable results
- **Fine readability** in grams, milligrams, and micrograms
- **Calibration support** to keep readings accurate and compliant
