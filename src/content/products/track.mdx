## Track weighing mobile weighbridge — economical weighing for lighter traffic

The **track weighing weighbridge** is a lighter, lower-cost member of our mobile
weighbridge range, designed for **four-wheeler vehicles**. By using a
track-style deck with **reduced structural weight**, it brings the cost down
while still delivering accurate weighing across **5 MT to 100 MT** — a practical
choice where you mainly weigh smaller vehicles and want to control budget.

## Why choose a track weighing weighbridge

- **Lower cost.** A lighter structure means a more economical weighbridge than a
  full-platform design.
- **Right-sized.** Purpose-built for four-wheeler vehicle weighing.
- **Relocatable.** Like our instant mobile weighbridges, it installs on a hard,
  level surface and can be moved between sites.
- **Quick to deploy.** Minimal site preparation gets weighing started fast.

## How it works

Vehicles are weighed on the track-style deck, whose load cells feed a
**pre-calibrated digital indicator**. The indicator processes and displays the
weight, ready to print, store, or pass to your weighing software — with optional
IP cameras and lightning protection available.

## Applications

The track weighing weighbridge suits construction sites, mines, and quarries —
and any operation that mainly weighs four-wheeler vehicles and wants a
cost-effective, relocatable solution.
