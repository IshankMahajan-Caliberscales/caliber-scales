## Logistics scales — weight and dimension in one step

Caliber logistics scales capture **both weight and dimensional (volumetric)
weight** in a single operation, helping logistics and courier businesses price
and bill accurately while cutting the **human error that comes from manual data
entry**. There is no need to calculate volumetric weight by hand — the system
measures it for you.

## Cubic Scan 3D dimensioning

Our **Cubic Scan** machine uses **3D scanning technology** — cameras and lasers
— to capture the dimensions and shape of an object in three-dimensional space,
producing a detailed dimensional model. Combined with the weight reading, it
gives you everything needed for volumetric pricing.

## Built for throughput and integration

- **Built-in 24-column DOT-matrix printer** for instant labels and dockets
- **PC integration** with external-device data transfer
- Designed to keep parcels and pallets moving through goods-in and dispatch
