## Instant mobile weighbridge — weighing that moves with you

An **instant mobile weighbridge** is a relocatable truck scale designed to be up
and running fast. Built on a **fabricated steel base frame with integrated load
cells**, it sits directly on any hard, smooth, level surface — **no concrete
foundation required** — and can typically be installed in just **4–6 hours**.
When your operation moves, the weighbridge can move with it.

Caliber Scales manufactures instant mobile weighbridges from **5 MT to 100 MT**,
giving project and seasonal sites full-capacity weighing without the cost and
delay of permanent civil work.

## Why choose an instant mobile weighbridge

- **No foundation.** The self-contained steel frame removes the need for a pit or
  concrete base — just a firm, level surface.
- **Fast to deploy.** Operational in around 4–6 hours, so weighing starts almost
  immediately.
- **Relocatable.** Move it between project sites, seasons, or locations as your
  needs change.
- **Lower upfront cost.** Minimal site preparation keeps initial spend down.

## How it works

The weighing deck and load cells are integrated into a portable steel base
frame. Once positioned and levelled on a hard surface, the load cells feed a
**pre-calibrated digital indicator** that processes and displays the weight,
ready to print or record.

## Applications

Instant mobile weighbridges are ideal for construction sites, mines and
quarries, seasonal agricultural operations, and any project where weighing is
temporary or the location changes over time.
