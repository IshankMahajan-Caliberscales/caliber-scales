## Industrial platform scales — portable, dormant, and custom

Caliber Scales manufactures robust **platform scales** for everyday industrial
weighing of drums, sacks, pallets, and bulk goods. From compact **portable**
models to large **dormant (floor) platforms**, each is built for accuracy and a
long service life — and available in mild steel or stainless steel to suit your
environment.

## Portable platform scales

Our portable platform scales weigh from **50 kg to 500 kg** and come in pan
sizes of **350×350, 400×400, 500×500, and 600×600 mm**. Battery or mains
operation makes them easy to position around a shop floor, warehouse, or loading
area, while a clear digital readout keeps weighing fast and accurate.

## Dormant (heavy-duty) platform scales

For heavier industrial loads, dormant platform scales use larger pans —
**800×800, 1000×1000, 1200×1200, and 1500×1500 mm** — set low to the floor for
easy loading of pallets and bulky goods.

## Customised platform scales

Need a non-standard size, capacity, or finish? We build **customised platform
scales** around your specific requirement, including stainless steel
construction for hygienic or corrosive environments.

## Why choose Caliber platform scales

- **Easy to use** with an accurate, clear digital display
- **Sturdy design** engineered for long, daily industrial use
- **Battery or mains** power for flexible placement
- **MS or SS** pans for standard or hygienic settings
- **Custom builds** for specialised needs
