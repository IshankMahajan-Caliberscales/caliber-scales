## Pit type weighbridge — flush, space-saving, and easy to approach

A **pit type weighbridge** (also called a **pit-mounted weighbridge**) is
installed flush with ground level inside an excavated pit, so the weighing deck
sits level with the surrounding surface. With no approach ramps, vehicles can
drive onto it from any side — which makes it the natural choice for yards where
space is tight or traffic flows from several directions.

Caliber Scales manufactures pit type weighbridges from **5 MT to 100 MT**,
engineered for accurate, dependable weighing in demanding industrial conditions.

## Why choose a pit type design

- **Saves space.** Without ramps, the weighbridge occupies only its deck
  footprint — ideal for compact or congested yards.
- **Level access from all sides.** A flush deck lets vehicles approach and leave
  from any direction, easing traffic flow at busy gates.
- **Low profile, low wear.** The ground-level deck reduces wear and keeps the
  site clean and unobtrusive.
- **Built to specification.** We engineer the pit, foundation, and drainage to
  match your vehicles and site conditions.

## How it works

The steel weighing deck rests on load cells set within the pit. As a vehicle
drives on, the load cells convert the weight into an electrical signal that a
**pre-calibrated digital indicator** processes and displays on an LED, GLCD, or
TFT screen — ready to print, store, or pass to your weighing software.

## Instrumentation and options

- Pre-calibrated indicators with intelligent terminals
- Connectivity over **RS232, RS485, Ethernet, and USB**
- IP cameras for weighment photo capture
- Vehicle-positioning guidance and weighbridge software
- Lightning protection for exposed installations

## Applications

Pit type weighbridges suit space-constrained logistics yards and plants in
cement, steel, and manufacturing — anywhere a flush, ramp-free approach is
preferred and the site can support proper pit drainage.

## Pit type vs surface mounted

If your site has drainage or groundwater concerns, a
[surface mounted weighbridge](/weighbridges/surface-mounted/) may be simpler. For
very heavy, continuous traffic, consider an [RCC weighbridge](/weighbridges/rcc/).
