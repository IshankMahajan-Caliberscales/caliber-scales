## Industrial crane scales for heavy suspended loads

Caliber Scales manufactures industrial **crane scales** that weigh a suspended
load in the air — used with overhead or EOT cranes, chains, and shackles. Built
for harsh environments, they feature a **large display readable from a
distance**, a **wireless remote with around 20 m range**, a **rechargeable
battery**, and a **dismountable hook** that fits standard lifting equipment.
Capacities run from **1,000 kg to 30,000 kg**.

## Heat-resistant crane scales

For heat-intensive processes such as steel and foundry work, our **heat-resistant
crane scales are protected up to 1600°C**, so you can weigh hot loads safely and
accurately near furnaces and casting areas.

## Why choose Caliber crane scales

- **Weigh suspended loads** without a platform
- **Large display** and **~20 m wireless remote**
- **Rechargeable battery** for mobile use
- **Robust** for harsh industrial conditions
- **Heat-resistant** models for foundry and steel use

## Applications

Crane scales suit workshops, fabrication, steel and foundries (heat-resistant),
warehousing, and scrap/heavy-goods handling. For lighter suspended weighing such
as LPG cylinders, see [hanging scales](/products/hanging-scales/), or
[request a quote](/contact/) for the right crane scale for your operation.
