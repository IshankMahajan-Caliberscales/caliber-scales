## RCC weighbridge — concrete strength for heavy, continuous use

An **RCC weighbridge** uses a deck of reinforced cement concrete cast on a steel
framework, in place of an all-steel platform. The concrete construction is
exceptionally durable under very heavy, continuous vehicle traffic and offers a
long service life with minimal maintenance — which is why RCC decks are a
favourite in mining, quarrying, and cement.

Caliber Scales manufactures RCC weighbridges from **5 MT to 100 MT**, available
in both **pitless and pit-mounted** configurations.

## Why choose an RCC deck

- **Built for heavy duty.** Reinforced concrete stands up to relentless
  high-tonnage traffic without the fatigue an all-steel deck can experience.
- **Long, low-maintenance life.** RCC decks typically need less upkeep and last
  longer in harsh conditions.
- **Corrosion resistance.** Concrete is naturally more resistant to corrosion
  than steel in dusty, abrasive, or chemically aggressive environments.
- **Your choice of installation.** Order it pitless or pit-mounted to suit your
  yard and drainage.

## How it works

The reinforced concrete deck rests on load cells; as a vehicle drives on, the
load cells convert the weight into a signal that a **pre-calibrated digital
indicator** processes and displays. Readings can be printed, recorded, and fed
into your weighbridge software.

## Instrumentation and options

- Pre-calibrated indicators with intelligent terminals
- Connectivity over **RS232, RS485, Ethernet, and USB**
- IP cameras, vehicle positioning, and weighbridge software
- Lightning protection for exposed sites

## Applications

RCC weighbridges are widely used in mining, quarrying, cement, and heavy industry
— anywhere weighbridges see constant, high-tonnage traffic and a long, robust
service life is essential.
