## Back in service, fast

A weighbridge or scale that's down means stalled vehicles, stalled despatch, and
lost time. As a manufacturer, we know these systems inside out — so we can
diagnose faults quickly and get you weighing again.

## What we repair

We diagnose and repair **load cells, junction boxes and wiring, indicators and
displays, and structural or deck issues**. Once the fault is fixed, we
**re-calibrate and verify** the system across its range before sign-off, so you
get back not just a working weighbridge but an accurate one.

- **Quality parts** for lasting repairs
- **Re-calibrated** and verified before handover
