## Turn weight data into workflow

A weight on a display is useful; a weight that flows automatically into your
ticketing, billing, ERP, and reports is far more so. Caliber Scales connects
weighbridge and scale data to your systems — and can automate the weighing
process itself.

## What we do

We start by **mapping your requirements** — what data, to which systems — then
**integrate** the indicators and software over the right interfaces (RS232,
RS485, Ethernet, USB). Where useful we add **automation**: ticketing, boom
barriers, RFID vehicle identification, and IP cameras for
[unmanned weighbridge](/weighbridges/unmanned/) operation. Finally we
**validate** end to end so data lands correctly.

## Why Caliber for software integration

- **Manufacturer integration** — we know our indicators and systems
- **ERP & reporting** — weights flow straight into your workflow
- **Automation** — ticketing, barriers, RFID, and unmanned operation
- **Fewer errors** — less manual entry, more auditable records
