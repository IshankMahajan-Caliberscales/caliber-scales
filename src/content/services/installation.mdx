## Installation done right, by the manufacturer

A weighbridge or scale is only as accurate as its installation. Get the
foundation, levelling, or wiring wrong and you'll fight accuracy and reliability
for years. Because Caliber Scales designs and builds the equipment, we know
exactly how it needs to be installed — and we do it ourselves.

## What we do

We start with a **site survey** to understand your vehicles or loads, ground
conditions, and layout. We then **specify the foundation and civil work** and
coordinate the groundwork, **install and wire** the equipment, and finally
**calibrate, test, and commission** it — briefing your team before handover.

For background on preparing a site, see our
[weighbridge civil & foundation guide](/resources/installation-guides/weighbridge-civil-foundation-guide/).
