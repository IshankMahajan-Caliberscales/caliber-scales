## Keep weighing accurate and available

When a weighbridge or scale goes down, it can stall an entire despatch or
production line. An **Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)** turns maintenance from
reactive to planned — scheduled servicing and calibration that protect accuracy
and uptime, with priority support if something does go wrong.

## What an AMC includes

After **assessing** your equipment and uptime needs, we set up **scheduled
preventive maintenance and calibration checks**, provide **priority support**
between visits, and keep **service and calibration records** for your compliance.

## Why an AMC from Caliber

- **Planned uptime** — preventive maintenance instead of breakdowns
- **Accuracy maintained** — regular calibration built in
- **Priority response** — faster help when you need it
- **Records** — documentation for audits and compliance
